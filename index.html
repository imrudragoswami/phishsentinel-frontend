How to Protect Yourself

Phishing attacks are getting smarter. Combine PhishSentinel's AI scanning with these essential cybersecurity habits.

Verify Senders

Always check the actual email address, not just the display name. Attackers often spoof trusted brands.

Enable MFA

Multi-Factor Authentication adds a critical layer of defense even if your password gets compromised.

Hover to Preview

Hover over links to preview the actual destination URL before you click. If in doubt, scan it here.

Beware of Urgency

Phishing emails often create a false sense of urgency (e.g., "Account closing!"). Take a breath and verify.
